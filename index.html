About Ninja-Cutter

Welcome to thutter. Ninja Cutter is an exciting and fast-paced game where you slice through targets with precision. Challenge yourself in different modes and aim for the highest score! Your objective: become a master of slicing targets! What better way to do that than play Ninja-cutter, the original hit boxes-slicing game?

Prove to your friends and family that you’re the top ninja around. Show them how much you can cut, or compare your score with your friends to rise through the ranks and reach the top of the leaderboard! Big fan of Ninja-cutter: Puss in Boots? Be sure to challenge yourself with the entertaining and addictive minigames and find your favourite!

What are you waiting for? Whether you're playing for some mindless fun and trying to relax and pass the time, or trying to improve your skills as a master ninja, this is the game for you. Enjoy the awesomeness and believe the hype of Ninja-Cutter – chop chop! ⚔️
